About Us

Tanzania Heritage Initiatives is a registered non-governmental oraganisation working to protect, conserve and promote the Heritage of Tanzania. The organisation was founded with the aim of advocating for Heritage Management in Tanzania and community inclusion in doing so.

We believe managing Heritage resources is a collective effort that should not leave out the concerned community.

We as an organisation strive to protect, conserve and promote Heritage as a holistic both cultural and natural. Similarly adressing issues related to impacts of climate change in Heritage Management and preserving, restoring and salvaging the at risk heritage.

Our Mission

Improving the standard of living of people through community inclusive on sustainable use, protection, conservation and developing heritage sites and natural resources

Our Vision

A community with healthy and stable development attained through protection and preservation of heritage sites and natural resources
